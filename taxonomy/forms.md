# Forms

A structured vocabulary of jewellery forms used in VelvetMatter Ontology.

### Anklet
Jewellery worn around the ankle.

### Bangle
Rigid circular bracelet without a clasp.

### Bracelet
Flexible or articulated ornament worn around the wrist.

### Brooch
Decorative pin with a fastening mechanism.

### Chain
Linked metal elements forming a flexible strand.

### Earrings
Ornaments worn on the ears.

### Hair Comb
Decorative comb used in hair styling.

### Hairpin
Slender pin used to secure hair.

### Locket
Pendant designed to hold a photograph or small object.

### Medallion
Large pendant, often commemorative.

### Necklace
Ornament worn around the neck.

### Pendant
Suspended ornament attached to a chain or necklace.

### Pin
Simple fastening ornament without a brooch clasp.

### Ring
Circular ornament worn on the finger.

### Sculpture
Three‑dimensional jewellery object or wearable art.

### Tiara
Decorative head ornament worn on the forehead or crown.

### Tie Clip
Horizontal clip used to secure a tie.

### Tie Pin
Vertical pin used to fasten a tie.
